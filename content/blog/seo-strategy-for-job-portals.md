## Why Job Portal SEO Is Different

Job portals deal with fast-changing content and thousands of similar pages. Without a strong system, pages compete with each other and waste crawl budget.

## Core SEO Framework

### 1. Build category clusters

Create pillar pages for major intent buckets such as government jobs, private jobs, admit cards, and results.

### 2. Standardize on-page templates

Use repeatable templates for titles, meta descriptions, and heading flow so every page is optimized by default.

### 3. Strengthen internal linking

Add contextual links between related jobs, exams, and eligibility guides to improve topical authority.

## Technical Foundations

### Improve Core Web Vitals

Optimize fonts, image delivery, and script loading. A fast page increases both user retention and ranking potential.

### Keep pages index-friendly

Avoid duplicate content, maintain canonical rules, and handle outdated listings with clear archival strategy.

## Content Strategy

Use intent-led keywords, answer common user questions, and keep eligibility, dates, and application steps clearly structured. This improves both engagement and search visibility.

## Final Takeaway

A durable **SEO strategy for job portals** depends on architecture, consistency, and speed. Teams that combine product UX with technical SEO usually outperform content-only competitors.
